Cole Palmer joined from Manchester City on transfer deadline day in September 2023.

Name: Cole Palmer
National Team: England
Place of Birth: Manchester, England
Position: Attacking Midfilder/winger
DOB: 6 may, 2002 (age 24)
Height: 1.85m (6ft 1in)
Debut: 2 September, 2023

A young attacking player of outstanding all-round quality who can create and score goals, his first year at Stamford Bridge could hardly have gone better as he was awarded Player of the Season and became a full England international. In July 2024, he scored for his nation in the European Championship final versus Spain.

Ahead of the start of the 2024/25 campaign, there was good news for Chelsea fans when Palmer signed a two-year contract extension, committing him to the club until 2033. Very soon after he was one of the nominees for the prestigious Ballon d'Or and he was ranked 25th for that award when the vote result was announced, with another nomination to follow a year later. This time he rose to eighth in the poll.
